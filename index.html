About Me

New York based Front End developer with almost 2 years of hands-on experience coding websites and applications using modern HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, jQuery, PHP, WordPress, JavaScript. I enjoy building websites and working with front end technologies. I design user friendly, intuitive interfaces allowing users to focus on the task. Have worked with Database systems(MySQL, MS SQL server) as well. Previously worked as a Web developer where I had built a responsive HR department website from scratch. When I'm not coding, you'll find me in the kitchen making Indian dishes(Chicken Biryani is my favorite). If you are a business seeking a web presence or an employer looking to hire, you can get in touch with me.
